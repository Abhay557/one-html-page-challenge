Movie List

This is a list of movies watched by Christopher Powroznik. Hopefully the goal one day is to include all movies watched. For sake of understanding, when rating the list, I consider 5 as the average movie, so trying to make things somewhat bell-curved. Also, I do not rate movies giving context for their timeframe, so no bonus points for being culturally significant or being old. I initially made the list from the top 250 on IMDB, but will expand upon over time.

If you want to add a comment or feedback, there is an array you can add to. Feel free to commit your comment back to the repo.
